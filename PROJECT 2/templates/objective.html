Project Objectives

  • To demonstrate the mathematical principles behind CT scan reconstruction
  • To visualize the Radon transform and its inverse in action
  • To provide an interactive tool for understanding how different projection angles affect image reconstruction
  • To illustrate the importance of sufficient angular sampling in CT imaging
  • To show the practical application of image processing techniques in medical imaging
